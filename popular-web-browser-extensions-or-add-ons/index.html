Popular web browser extensions or add-ons

The majority of these highly-rated extensions are available on Chrome, Firefox and Microsoft Edge.  Apple's Safari browser has  it's own recommendations.

Security & Privacy
Extension (Link)Primary FunctionKey Features (Free Tier)
uBlock OriginAd & Tracker Blocker (Open Source)All browsers - Blocks intrusive ads, pop-ups, and malware domains with low CPU/memory usage for faster browsing.
GhosteryAnti-Tracking & Ad BlockerAll browsers - Identifies and blocks tracking technologies on a webpage, allowing you to see who is tracking you.
Privacy BadgerAlgorithmic Tracker BlockerAll browsers - Automatically learns and blocks invisible tracking domains based on their behavior, without relying on preset lists.
AdBlock Plus (ABP)Ad & Pop-up BlockerAll browsers - One of the original ad blockers; blocks video ads, pop-ups, and banners, supporting acceptable ads by default.
DuckDuckGo PrivacyPrivacy Dashboard & Tracker BlockerAll browsers - Blocks third-party trackers, enforces encryption, and shows a Privacy Grade on every website you visit.
ClearURLsTracking URL RemoverFirefox, Chrome & Edge -  Automatically removes tracking elements from URLs to clean links and enhance privacy.
— Safari-Specific Free Alternatives —
GhosteryAnti-Tracking & Ad BlockerIdentifies and blocks tracking technologies on a webpage, allowing you to see who is tracking you.
AdGuardAdvanced Ad & Tracker BlockerUses Apple's Content Blocking API for efficient blocking of banners, video ads, and most trackers in the free version.
AdBlockAd and Pop-up BlockerA powerful, free content blocker that stops pop-ups and video ads; offers customizable filter lists.
DuckDuckGo PrivacyPrivacy Dashboard & Tracker BlockerBlocks third-party trackers, enforces encryption, and shows a Privacy Grade on every website you visit.
