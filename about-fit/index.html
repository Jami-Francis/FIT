About Francis IT

FIT provides expert IT solutions for large homes, small businesses, and SOHO clients. We specialize in seamless connectivity and storage, offering services like secure Wi-Fi printing, simple Network Attached Storage (NAS) solutions, and whole-home Wi-Fi with mesh networking to eliminate dead spots. No job is too small - we're happy to set up a new laptop, desktop, or printer on your existing network or troubleshoot any problems you're having. Give us a call to discuss your needs; your initial consultation is free. For a complete list of our services, please visit the Services page.  FIT is based in Borrego Springs, CA and offers support to some outlying areas.  PO Box 2646, 92004

Mr. Francis is passionate about tech and takes pride in his ability to explain problems and solutions in simple terms. He has spoken to Rotary clubs and private functions in the Memphis and San Diego area. As the grandson of Greek immigrants, he traveled widely in Europe and Asia, yet greatly values the American experience and its promise.

FIT (formerly Coronado Computer Service) moved to the San Diego area in 2011 and to Borrego Springs in 2016. The owner, Jami Francis, was previously a Senior Systems Analyst with International Paper at their corporate headquarters in Memphis, Tennessee until 2003. With over 30 years of network administration, WAN/LAN security and IT consulting, his business and he have served small and large companies in the east Memphis area since 1998, and have done work for PakMail, Batteries Plus, Zoccola & Kaplan Accounting, Budget Blinds, Browning Construction, Paradigm Realty Advisors, Smith Barney (now Morgan Stanley Wealth Mgmt.), FedEx and Schering-Plough (now Merck & Co.)
