About Francis IT

FIT is geared toward small business and SOHO clients. We are also pleased to offer concierge residential services including Wi-Fi printing, simple network storage solutions, Wi-Fi using MIMO networking via ethernet over powerline and Wi-Fi NAS. No job is to small...   we are glad to set up a new laptop, desktop or printer to your existing home/office network or troubleshoot any problems you might be having. You are invited to give us a call and discuss your needs; your initial consultation is free. Please see the Services page for more details.

Mr. Francis is passionate about tech and takes pride in his ability to explain problems and solutions in simple terms. He has spoken to Rotary clubs and private functions in the Memphis and San Diego area. As the grandson of Greek immigrants, he traveled widely in Europe and Asia, yet greatly values the American experience and its promise.

FIT (formerly Coronado Computer Service) moved to the San Diego area in 2011 and to Borrego Springs in 2016. The owner, Jami Francis, was previously a Senior Systems Analyst with International Paper at their corporate headquarters in Memphis, Tennessee until 2003. With over 25 years of network administration, WAN/LAN security and IT consulting, his business and he have served small and large companies in the east Memphis area since 1998, and have done work for PakMail, Batteries Plus, Zoccola & Kaplan Accounting, Budget Blinds, Browning Construction, Paradigm Realty Advisors, Smith Barney (now Morgan Stanley Wealth Mgmt.), FedEx and Schering-Plough (now Merck & Co.)
