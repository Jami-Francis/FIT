Borrego Springs  Mac & Windows Support

Francis IT offers full support for Macs, Windows computers and most peripherals

If your computer / server has a Trojan horse, worm, malware (pop-ups, etc) that seem like they cannot be removed - we have the expertise to clean your system, making sure nothing is left behind to cause future problems.

Maybe you have a new Wi-Fi router, computer or printer you would like installed and configured. Or your tablet or smartphone will not connect to your printer like it used to. We can help! Are you interested in expanding your Wi-Fi network? Wi-Fi extenders are not the answer, Mesh Wi-Fi with a MoCA or Ethernet over Powerline backhaul is!

Often computers are slow due to a combination of factors, including software bloat, hardware limitations, and accumulated junk. Software updates and installations (apps) can increase the demands on a computer's resources, while outdated hardware may struggle from old drivers, when newer drivers or firmware are actually available. Repetitive popup windows while on the internet can make a computer or phone impossible to use. We understand! Give us a call to discuss your particular situation. 

Francis IT (FIT) can offer hardware and software solutions to meet your needs. Whether it's a new iMac, MacBook Air, Pro or mini, or any Windows laptop or desktop, tablet or cellphone - we will set it up and customize it's applications for you. We do not offer in-house screen repairs. Older computers running MacOS X or Windows 8.x or 10 may just need additional memory and a new SSD to make then run like new.

Windows 10 will reach "End of Life" on October 14, 2025, meaning Microsoft will officially stop providing free technical assistance, feature updates, and crucial security updates for Windows 10. If you're upgrading, we can back up your data and transfer it to a new computer or if you want to keep your current PC, FIT can do an in-place upgrade from Windows 10 to Windows 11 allowing you to update your operating system while keeping your personal files, apps, and settings intact. FIT has been very successful in upgrading top of the line, older PCs, to Windows 11 Home or Pro even though they do not meet the hardware requirements for a Win 11 upgrade.

Consultations are free and surprisingly informative. We may discover options you didn't know you had. We can also help you set up your DVR or NVR security system and  we are also happy to set up any peripherals - Wi-Fi printers, Smart TVs or webcams - to work with your current system and show you how to use them.
