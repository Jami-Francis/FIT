* The version number jumped from macOS 15 (Sequoia) to macOS 26 (Tahoe) because Apple decided to unify and standardize the major version number across all its operating systems (macOS, iOS & iPadOS). The new version number, "26", was chosen to align with the year following the release of the OS, similar to how car models are named. This makes it simpler to track the current generation of Apple software across all devices.
