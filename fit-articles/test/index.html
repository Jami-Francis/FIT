Popular web browser extensions or add-ons

The majority of these highly-rated extensions are available across the major modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox and Microsoft Edge.  Apple's Safari browser has  it's own recommendations.

Security & Privacy
Extension (Link)Primary FunctionKey Features (Free Tier)
uBlock OriginAd & Tracker Blocker (Open Source)Blocks intrusive ads, pop-ups, and malware domains with low CPU/memory usage for faster browsing.
GhosteryAnti-Tracking & Ad BlockerIdentifies and blocks tracking technologies on a webpage, allowing you to see who is tracking you.
Privacy BadgerAlgorithmic Tracker BlockerAutomatically learns and blocks invisible tracking domains based on their behavior, without relying on preset lists.
AdBlock Plus (ABP)Ad & Pop-up BlockerOne of the original ad blockers; blocks video ads, pop-ups, and banners, supporting acceptable ads by default.
HTTPS EverywhereSecurity Protocol EnforcerEncrypts your communications with many major websites, making your browsing more secure by enforcing the HTTPS protocol.
ClearURLsTracking URL RemoverAutomatically removes tracking elements (like `?utm_source=...`) from URLs to clean links and enhance privacy.
— Safari-Specific Free Alternatives —
AdGuard for SafariAdvanced Ad & Tracker BlockerUses Apple's Content Blocking API for efficient blocking of banners, video ads, and most trackers in the free version.
AdBlock for SafariAd and Pop-up BlockerA powerful, free content blocker that stops pop-ups and video ads; offers customizable filter lists.
DuckDuckGo PrivacyPrivacy Dashboard & Tracker BlockerBlocks third-party trackers, enforces encryption, and shows a Privacy Grade on every website you visit.

Momentum (App Store)New Tab DashboardThe official Safari version provides a new tab page with a daily focus, to-do list, and inspirational quotes (free tier).

StopTheMadness Pro (Free Features)Browser Feature RestorerRestores essential browser features on restrictive websites (e.g., right-click, copy/paste, drag & drop) that sites often disable.
