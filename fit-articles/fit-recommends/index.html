FIT Recommends

For the most part current version of both Windows and the Mac OS will keep the average user safe from Malware.  If, however, you have inquisitive teenagers or employees, that might browse to risky websites offering free apps, games, music or porn, please consider Malwarebytes for Mac or PC. FIT offers a discounted price for a one year license.

For excellent FREE general cleanup and a potentially faster system, please consider OnyX or cCleaner for Mac.  For Windows, use the new free PC Manager app from Microsoft which runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 systems.  PowerToys from Microsoft adds utilities for power users to tune and streamline their Windows experience for greater productivity. 

Familiarize yourself with the Security features of your computer!
